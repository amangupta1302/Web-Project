What is MyNotes?

It is a notes portal for students of SRM Institute of Science and Technology,
mainly from the IT and CSE department.
Students will be able to access different notes as well as question papers for multiple courses.

Main Courses

  • DBMS
  • Computer Networks
  • Operating Systems
  • Microprocessors and Microcontrollers
  • Python
  • Data Structures
